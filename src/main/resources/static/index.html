01Philosophy

Two seas, one stillness.

In Kyoto, chef Tanaka was taught to listen for the moment rice stops speaking. In Dubrovnik, he found the same silence between waves lapping against stone.

Kyoto Cuisine is a counter of twelve seats where those two silences meet — Hokkaido uni beside Ston oyster, Tsugaru mackerel cured in Pag salt, wasabi grated on Dalmatian stone.
